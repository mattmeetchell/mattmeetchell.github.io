A Little About Me

I’m a multi-disciplinary graphic designer located in Manhattan, New York (wait not Brooklyn?!?) specializing in web and user interface design. I received a degree in Graphic and Interactive Communications ( dramatic right?) in 2013 from Ringling College of Art and Design. Since then I’ve been continuing to hone my skills and attempt to create stuff that’s worth using. This being said, if you like what you see…I’m open to freelance…feel free to reach out!
